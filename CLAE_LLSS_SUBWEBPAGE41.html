LPU-Legal Studies Society (LLSS)

LLSS is the official student organization for Bachelor of Arts in Legal Studies, dedicated to fostering a fair and equitable community and expanding legal knowledge.


The home and governing body organization of the Bachelor of Arts in Legal Studies of the Lyceum of the Philippines University – Cavite.

As the sole official student organization of LPU Cavite’s Legal Studies majors, the organization’s leading platform is to create a fair and equitable community, develop, and expand its knowledge and capabilities for all Legal Studies students in line with the legal field.

This organization will serve as a forum for aspiring lawyers-among others in the program to exchange knowledge and views on topics of interest in the country’s legal system.

The LLSS is committed to supporting and providing meaningful projects, which include academic and interactive seminars, event collaborations, and projects for career networking with legal professionals.

This organization vowed to strengthen the interaction between the university, the law, professors, and society.
