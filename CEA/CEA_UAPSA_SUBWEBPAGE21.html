United Architects of the Philippines Student Auxiliary – Lyceum of the Philippines University Cavite Chapter (UAPSA-LPUC)


Established in 1989 to cater for BS Architecture students, UAPSA is the foremost and duly recognized student organization of United Architects of the Philippines (UAP).

The organization’s objectives include providing a reliable community, giving students a chance to expand their reach and network with fellow architecture students, and building a platform to widen knowledge and experience.
