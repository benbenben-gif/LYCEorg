Junior Philippine Institute of Accountants (JPIA)


One of the college-based student organizations in the Lyceum of the Philippines University – Manila which was also then adopted by LPU Cavite.

It is a Bonafide member of JPIA National and Regional Chapter as well as its Cavite chapter.

The organization renew its membership in the above-mentioned organizations annually and is an active member since its establishment in 2008.
