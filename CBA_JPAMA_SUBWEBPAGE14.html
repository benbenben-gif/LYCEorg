Junior Philippine Association of Management Accountants (JPAMA)


Junior Philippine Association of Management Accountants (JPAMA): A student organization composed of Bachelor of Science in Management Accounting (BSMA) students under the College of Business Administration at Lyceum of the Philippines University – Cavite.

It is established to build a dynamic and supportive community where members can actively participate in activities that enhance learning beyond the classroom.

JPAMA aims to provide opportunities for academic enrichment, leadership development, and professional readiness, equipping its members for future careers in the field of management accounting.
