Awakened Circle of Elite Students (ACES)

ACES is a student organization dedicated to developing CITHM students in their respective areas of specialization through various skill-sharpening activities.


ACES was founded by Ms. Gianna Paula Garcia and her colleagues under the supervision of Dr. Andrew Nico O. Pilapil. On 18th of May 2017, it was formally approved by the CITHM Dean, Dr. Mark Irvin C. Cells and Ms. Lourdes C. Reyes of Student Affairs Office.

Its purpose is to spearhead the development of CITHM students in their respective areas of specialization by engaging in various activities that aims to sharpen their skills and for the furtherance of their expertise not limited to their designated field.
