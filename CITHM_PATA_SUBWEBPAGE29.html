Pacific Asia Travel Association – LPU Cavite Student Chapter (PATA)

PATA is an international organization for International Tourism Management students, providing a platform for skill development, networking, and leadership in the travel and tourism industry.


An international organization under the College of International Tourism and Hospitality Management of Lyceum of Philippines University – Cavite, open to all Bachelor of Science in International Tourism Management students.

It provides a platform for members to develop their skills, expand professional networks, and grow as future leaders in the travel and tourism industry.

The organization’s membership offers a variety of learning experiences for its members–allowing participation in events and seminars both within and outside the university and the opportunity to contribute to meaningful initiatives in the tourism industry.

It fosters personal and professional growth by encouraging collaboration, leadership, and innovation among its members.

Through these experiences, students gain valuable insights and connections that prepare them for future roles in the global travel and tourism sector.
