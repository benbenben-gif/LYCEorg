Philippine Society of Medical Technology Students (PHISMETS)

LPU Cavite Chapter is a community of passionate and driven Medical Technology students from Lyceum of the Philippines University – Cavite. As a student-led organization, PHISMETS provides a supportive space where future medical technologists can grow together academically, professionally, and personally. Through educational activities, leadership opportunities, outreach programs, and meaningful collaborations, the organization helps members build their skills, strengthen their values, and prepare for the real-world challenges of the healthcare field. More than just an organization, PHISMETS is a family that encourages excellence, integrity, and compassion in every aspiring MedTech student.
