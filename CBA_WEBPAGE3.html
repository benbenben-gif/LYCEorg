College of Business Administration (CBA)

The College of Business Administration is home to dynamic student organizations that empower future business leaders through experiential learning, collaboration, and innovation. These organizations provide opportunities for students to apply classroom knowledge in real-world settings.


The College of Business Administration (CBA) offers a vibrant and diverse array of student organizations that play a crucial role in shaping the next generation of business leaders. These organizations are designed to go beyond traditional classroom learning, providing students with practical, hands-on experiences.

By participating in these groups, students have the chance to apply theoretical concepts learned in lectures to real-world business scenarios. Furthermore, these organizations foster a strong sense of collaboration, encouraging students from different backgrounds and disciplines to work together.

In addition to professional development, student organizations within the CBA promote entrepreneurship and innovation. Many groups focus on business start-ups, social enterprises, and creative ventures, giving students the freedom to experiment with new ideas in a supportive environment.
