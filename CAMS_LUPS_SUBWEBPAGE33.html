League of United Psychology Students (LUPS)

The League of United Psychology Students (LUPS) is an organization dedicated to fostering academic excellence, professional development, and community engagement among psychology students. We aim to provide a supportive environment for students to explore various fields of psychology, enhance their skills, and connect with peers and professionals.


The League of United Psychology Students (LUPS) is an organization dedicated to fostering academic excellence, professional development, and community engagement among psychology students.

We aim to provide a supportive environment for students to explore various fields of psychology, enhance their skills, and connect with peers and professionals.

LUPS organizes workshops, seminars, and outreach programs to enrich the learning experience and promote the application of psychological principles in real-world settings.
