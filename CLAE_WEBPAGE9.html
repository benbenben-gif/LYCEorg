College of Liberal Arts and Education (CLAE)

The College of Liberal Arts and Education (CLAE) is home to dynamic student organizations that empower future educators, communicators, and social leaders through experiential learning, collaboration, and creativity. These organizations provide opportunities for students to apply classroom knowledge in real-world settings, develop leadership and communication skills, and foster a spirit of social responsibility, innovation, and professionalism.


The College of Liberal Arts and Education (CLAE) at Lyceum of the Philippines University–Cavite is home to a vibrant array of student organizations that nurture future educators, communicators, artists, and social leaders. These organizations play a pivotal role in complementing classroom learning by offering students practical, hands-on experiences that foster intellectual growth, personal development, and professional readiness. Through active participation, students are able to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world contexts, whether by organizing community outreach programs, facilitating educational workshops, engaging in creative and cultural projects, or participating in research and advocacy initiatives.

CLAE student organizations provide platforms for students to develop essential leadership and interpersonal skills. Members often assume key roles in planning events, leading committees, and collaborating on projects, which cultivates abilities in communication, teamwork, problem-solving, and decision-making. These experiences prepare students to take on challenges in their future careers, whether in education, media and communication, social work, or the arts, while also instilling confidence, initiative, and a professional mindset. Beyond leadership development, CLAE student organizations encourage creativity, innovation, and social responsibility. Many groups organize activities that promote literacy, community engagement, environmental awareness, and cultural appreciation, giving students the chance to make a tangible impact on society. Through these initiatives, students learn the importance of empathy, ethical practice, and civic responsibility, equipping them to become socially conscious professionals who understand the broader context of their work.

Overall, student organizations within CLAE at LPU–Cavite serve as an essential bridge between academic knowledge and professional practice. They provide a supportive environment where students can grow intellectually, socially, and personally, while cultivating leadership, creativity, professionalism, and a commitment to community service. By engaging in these organizations, CLAE students are empowered to become compassionate, innovative, and socially responsible leaders who are well-prepared to make meaningful contributions to their fields and society.
