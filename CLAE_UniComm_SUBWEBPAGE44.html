United Communicators (UniComm)


The premier organization for Bachelor of Arts in Communication students at the Lyceum of the Philippines University Cavite aspires to bridge the gap between Industry and academia.

This organization alms to foster connections and collaborations not only within their own institution but also with other universities.

Through these efforts, it seeks to create a dynamic and integrative learning environment that keeps pace with the evolving field of communication.
