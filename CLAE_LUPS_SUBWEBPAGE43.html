League of United Psychology Students (LUPS)

Established in 2014, LUPS is a student-led organization under the College of Liberal Arts and Education, supporting academic growth and well-being of Psychology students.


Established in 2014, is a student-lead organization under the College of Liberal Arts and Education.

It aims to support the academic growth and overall well-being of Psychology students through meaningful projects and activities.

LUPS is composed of three sub-organizations: the Documentation Division, the Visual and Performing Arts Division, and the Extension and Outreach Division.
