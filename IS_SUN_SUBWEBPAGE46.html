Students of United Nations

This organization supports international students at LPU-Cavite, fostering friendly relations and promoting diversity and shared objectives.


This organization is created for international students who are enrolled in the Lyceum of the Philippines University- Cavite campus in order to support and help them study in the Philippines and to promote friendly relations between the international students and Filipino student in order to appreciate diversity and share common objectives.
