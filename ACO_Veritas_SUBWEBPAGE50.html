THE PIONEER

The Pioneer, the official student publication of Lyceum of the Philippines University–Cavite, is dedicated to developing skilled, responsible, and socially aware campus journalists who document and interpret the events, issues, and stories that shape university life. Serving as the primary platform for campus news and student expression, The Pioneer provides rigorous training in news writing, feature writing, editorial commentary, photojournalism, layout design, and digital reporting. Through a balance of journalistic principles and hands-on practice, the organization equips aspiring writers and media practitioners with the knowledge and experience needed to navigate the fast-paced and ever-evolving field of modern journalism.
