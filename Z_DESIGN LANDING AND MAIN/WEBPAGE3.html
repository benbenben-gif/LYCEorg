College of Business Administration (CBA)

The College of Business Administration aims to develop student organizations that empower future business leaders through experiential learning, collaboration, and innovation. We encourage participation in real-world settings, enhance academic and communication skills, and foster a spirit of entrepreneurship and professionalism.
