Lyceum of the Philippines University-Cavite Association of Customs Administration Students (LACAS)

An exclusive course related student organization focusing on activities involving customs and tariff laws. It serves as the Cavite Chapter of the PSCAS National, and it delivers the intended academic competence and interpersonal skills of the Customs Administration students of LPU-Cavite Campus.
