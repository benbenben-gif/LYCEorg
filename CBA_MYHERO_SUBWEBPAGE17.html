Maven Youth of Human Resources, Entrepreneurs, Real Estates & Operations (MYHERO)

MYHERO is a dynamic and forward-thinking organization dedicated to shaping students’ academic and professional success during their college journey, focusing on Human Resource Development Management, Entrepreneurship, Real Estate Management, and Operations Management. Our purpose is clear: to empower academic excellence, foster innovations, and community engagement in students’ career preparations and their journey in their college life. We believe that every student is a potential Maven, capable of remarkable achievements, and it’s our mission to guide them on this business path.
