Lycean Pre-service Teachers’ Club (LPT)

LPT is a college-based organization for Teacher Education students, dedicated to academic and co-curricular advancement and fostering essential teaching skills.


A college-based organization duly established for the academic and co-curricular advancement of the Teacher Education (Bachelor of Early Childhood Education & Bachelor of Secondary Education) students of the Lyceum of the Philippines University Cavite.

This organization aims to provide Teacher Education students with the opportunities to share essential knowledge and skills that are relevant to Teacher Education students and prospective teachers.

Furthermore, we hope that through acquire in-depth understanding of the implications of various education-related concerns, issues, and challenges necessary for 21st century learners and school stakeholders while maintaining camaraderie and healthy relationships with one another.
