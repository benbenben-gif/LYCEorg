College of Information Technology and Computer Science (CCS)

The College of Computer Studies (CCS) is dedicated to developing innovative, competent, and industry-ready professionals in the fields of information technology, computer science, and digital innovation. The college provides a strong foundation in programming, systems analysis, software development, and emerging technologies through a balance of theoretical instruction and practical application. With a focus on creativity, problem-solving, and ethical computing, CCS prepares students to meet the demands of the rapidly evolving digital world.


The College of Computer Studies (CCS) at Lyceum of the Philippines University – Cavite is committed to shaping innovative, competent, and industry-ready professionals in the fields of Information Technology, Computer Science, and Digital Innovation. Recognizing the rapid evolution of technology and the increasing demand for skilled digital professionals, CCS provides a robust curriculum that combines theoretical foundations with practical, hands-on experience. Students gain comprehensive training in areas such as programming, systems analysis, software engineering, network administration, cybersecurity, and emerging technologies like artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and data analytics.

A key emphasis of the college is on cultivating creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills, enabling students to tackle real-world challenges with innovative solutions. Alongside technical skills, CCS promotes ethical computing and professional responsibility, ensuring that graduates are not only proficient in technology but also mindful of its impact on society, security, and privacy. By integrating projects, internships, research, and competitions into the learning experience, CCS prepares students to thrive in dynamic industries and to continuously adapt to technological advancements.

Beyond academics, the College of Computer Studies recognizes the importance of holistic development, which is where student organizations play a vital role. These organizations provide platforms for students to explore interests, develop leadership abilities, foster collaboration, and engage with the broader professional and academic community. Participation in student organizations helps students complement their technical training with interpersonal, organizational, and entrepreneurial skills.
