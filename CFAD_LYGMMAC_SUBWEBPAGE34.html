Lyceum Group of Multimedia Arts and Creatives (LYGMMAC)

LYGMMAC is a dynamic student organization from the College of Fine Arts and Design (CFAD) dedicated to fostering excellence and leadership within the student community.


A vibrant and purpose-driven student organization from the College of Fine Arts and Design (CFAD) dedicated to fostering excellence and leadership within the student community.

With an unwavering commitment to making a meaningful impact, LYGMMAC envisions a legacy that resonates through the students, the college, and the university.

At the core of our mission is a profound dedication to nurturing the passions and aspirations of our fellow Bachelor of Multimedia Arts students.

LYGMMAC serves as an avenue for student artists to realize their creative potential, providing a supportive environment where they can refine their artistic talents and find their artistic purpose both within the university and the community.

Our purpose is multi-faceted and essential to the development of both individual skills and collective artistic growth.

Through a range of activities and initiatives, we are dedicated to promoting multimedia arts both as an academic discipline and a form of creative expression.
