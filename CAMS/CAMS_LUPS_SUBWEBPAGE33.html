League of United Psychology Students (LUPS)


The new home of future Radiologic Technologists under the College of Allied Medical Sciences.

More than just an academic sub-organization, RADiance Society is a student-led movement that uplifts, connects, and empowers Radiologic Technology students by means of shared experiences and a shared passion for the art and science of medical imaging.Built on the idea that we, too, can be a source of radiance in the medical field, RADiance Society creates a safe space where learning goes beyond the four walls of the lecture room.

From course-aligned activities like educational seminars, themed events, and collaborative learning experiences, RADiance Society helps its members grow not solely as students, but as future professionals who learn with heart.RADiance Society believes that the Radiologic Technology journey goes beyond just board exams—it is about becoming professionals guided by LPU’s core values.

Dedicated to excellence in the service of God and country, our student organization aims to support Radiologic Technology students through every step of their academic journey and a deep sense of purpose in the allied health field.
