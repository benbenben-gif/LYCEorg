Foreign Service Council (FSC)


The official academic and student-led organization representing the voice, vision, and values of the Foreign Service Community.

With a strong commitment to excellence in diplomacy, global understanding, and civic responsibility, the Council fosters a vibrant platform for leadership, international awareness, and student development.

Guided by its core pillars – Cooperation, Unity and Peace – the Council promotes collaboration among students, faculty, and the broader global community.

Through its initiatives, programs, and events, the Council cultivates cultural sensitivity, critical thinking, and the spirit of public service.

Whether in academic discourse or community engagement, the FSC upholds its mission of shaping globally minded leaders ready to contribute meaningfully to botch local and international landscapes.
