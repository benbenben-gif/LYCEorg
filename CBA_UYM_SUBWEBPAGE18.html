Union of Young Marketers (UYM)


The official non-partisan, non-political, non-sectarian, and non-profit student organization of all Marketing Management students under the College of Business Administration at Lyceum of the Philippines University – Cavite.

It serves as the student governing body that represents the Marketing student community and upholds the university’s academic standards and core values of virtue, excellence, and service. UYM is composed of an Executive Board, permanent committees, and general members.

It functions as a democratic body that promotes leadership, unity, and the advancement of marketing knowledge through academic, civic, and social initiatives.

The organization also maintains active affiliation with the Philippine Junior Marketing Association (PJMA) to ensure its connection with national-level marketing student networks.
